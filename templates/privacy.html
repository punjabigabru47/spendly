Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login times and pages visited, used to maintain the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display your expense history and generate spending summaries.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces for the interface. Google may log your IP address when fonts are fetched.

We do not integrate with payment processors, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may do this from within your account settings, or by contacting us directly.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on this site.
